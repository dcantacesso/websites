CIAO

Founded in 1980, as a small residential plastering company, Joe Azzarello has grown his business over the years to be one of New England's specialty plaster companies. We specialize in all phases of plaster, acoustical plaster finishes, as well as a polished plaster line.

Owned and operated by Joe Azzarello since 1981, we have gone to great strides to ensure the highest quality of work, and are committed to meeting the needs of the customer.

MISSION

At Joe Azzarello Plastering, Inc . we understand what it takes to complete projects on time and under challenging budgets, while maintaining the highest quality of work. Our staff has extensive experience in large scale projects, and are capable of performing all duties necessary to ensure the project is a success.
